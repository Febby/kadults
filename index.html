What is Krav Maga?

Krav Maga is the official system of hand-to-hand combat and self-defense employed by the Israeli Defense Forces- (IDF), Mossad, and Shin Bet. Krav Maga training focuses on teaching efficient techniques which are specifically catered to reality based attack situations and specifically designed to get quick results in a short training period.
